How to Play

Character Movement

  • A to walk left
  • D to walk right
  • SPACE to jump
  • Hold SHIFT to run (when running, the player's jump force and speed doubles)

Game Enemies and Objects

  • Be careful with the clouds with faces, if you pass over one you will lose a life; You have 3 lives per game.
  • Each coin collected adds 10 points to your total score.
  • If you touch a lucky block you gain 50 score; It can happen up to 3 times per lucky block.
  • If you touch a chest and have collected more than half the coins you gain 100 score and the game ends; 2 possible endings.
  • Use the various power-ups to help you complete the game.
